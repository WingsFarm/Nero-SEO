# NERO Image Guidelines

## Brand Aesthetic
Images must feel like NERO: minimal, dark, masculine, intentional.
No clutter. No noise. Every element in the frame earns its place.

## Color Palette
- **Backgrounds:** Deep charcoal, near-black, dark concrete, matte black surfaces
- **Accents:** Cool silver, warm gold, ocean blue, dark teal
- **Avoid:** White backgrounds, bright colors, pastels, gradients

## Lighting
- Low-key, directional lighting — creates depth and shadow
- Metallic surfaces should catch light naturally — avoid overexposed glare
- Cinematic, moody feel — not studio-flat

## Composition
- Minimal — one or two subjects maximum
- Generous negative space around the subject
- Clean lines, geometric layouts
- Close-up details preferred over wide lifestyle shots

## Subject Style
- Jewelry on skin: wrist, neck, hand — natural, unposed
- Jewelry on surface: dark stone, concrete, brushed metal, dark wood
- No artificial props, flowers, or decorative distractions

## Model Direction (when applicable)
- Male model, age 25–40
- Calm, confident expression — not smiling or posed
- Minimal clothing visible — focus stays on the jewelry
- No busy backgrounds or street scenes

## Collections — Visual Tone Per Collection
- **OCEAN GEMS:** Deep ocean blue tones, wet stone, subtle reflections
- **WILD SPIRIT:** Raw textures, dark earth, natural stone surfaces
- **ESSENCE OF GOD:** Pure black background, sharp metallic reflections, architectural feel
- **MINIMALIST:** Completely bare — black or charcoal background, one product, strong shadow

## What to Avoid
- Stock photo aesthetics
- Overly bright or saturated images
- Happy/casual lifestyle vibes
- Flower petals, candles, or feminine props
- Generic "jewelry on velvet" shots
- AI-default smooth gradients

## DALL-E Prompt Structure
Build prompts in this order:
1. Subject — what the jewelry is and how it's presented
2. Surface/background — material and color
3. Lighting — mood and direction
4. Style — photorealistic, editorial, close-up macro
5. Mood — one word (e.g., "minimal", "cinematic", "raw")

**Example prompt:**
> Close-up of a minimalist stainless steel bracelet resting on dark wet concrete, side lighting with subtle metallic reflection, editorial photography, cinematic, minimal

## Image Specs
- Format: JPG or WebP
- Minimum width: 1200px
- Blog header: 1792x1024
- Save to: `context/posts/{slug}/images/`
