# NERO Brand Voice Guidelines

## Brand Identity
NERO is a premium minimalist men's jewelry brand.
- Materials: 316L stainless steel
- Aesthetic: Ocean-inspired, clean, modern
- Values: Strength, self-expression, everyday durability, timeless design

## Language
- All blog posts must be written in **natural, fluent Hebrew**
- URL slugs must be in **English** (lowercase, hyphen-separated)
- Meta titles may include the NERO brand name when relevant

## Tone
- Confident
- Clean
- Modern
- Masculine but refined
- Strong, calm, and intentional — like the brand itself

## What to Avoid
- Slang
- Overly dramatic language
- Cheap marketing phrases
- Emojis
- Exaggerated hype
- AI-generic filler phrases:
  - "In today's fast-paced world"
  - "It is important to note"
  - "In conclusion"
  - "In today's digital age"

## Writing Style Rules
- Short sentences over long complex ones
- Active voice always
- Avoid repetition
- No keyword stuffing
- Writing must feel human, intentional, and editorial — not AI-generated

## Brand Positioning — Reinforce These in Every Post
- Style awareness
- Confidence
- Simplicity
- Quality craftsmanship
- Timeless design
- Minimalist masculinity

## Product Integration (Subtle Only)
The blog is **not** a sales page. When mentioning NERO products:
- Mention naturally and contextually
- Suggest product types: bracelets, chains, rings
- Focus on how they complement style

**Good example:**
> A minimalist stainless steel bracelet adds structure without overpowering your outfit.

**Never write:**
> Buy now before it sells out.

## Internal Linking
- Link to relevant NERO collections when natural
- Link to related blog posts
- Use descriptive anchor text, e.g.:
  - "minimalist men's bracelets"
  - "stainless steel chain necklaces"
- Never force links — only when contextually relevant

## Closing Every Post
Each post must end with:
1. A short 2–3 paragraph summary
2. A soft, brand-aligned closing
3. Optional subtle CTA, e.g.:
   > Explore NERO's collection of minimalist men's jewelry designed for everyday strength and style.

Never use aggressive or urgent CTAs.

## Approved Content Topics
- Men's style guides
- Jewelry styling advice
- Minimalism in fashion
- Masculinity and self-expression
- Outfit combinations
- Jewelry care and durability
- Stainless steel jewelry benefits
- Layering chains
- Bracelet stacking
- Ring styling
- Ocean-inspired fashion

**Do not** write about unrelated lifestyle content.
